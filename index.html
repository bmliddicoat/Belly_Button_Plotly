Improbable Beef, a food start-up, is searching for bacteria that could be used for meat substitute. A hypothesis has been developed that this potential microorganism on found in a bellybutton. Volunteers have agreed to have their bellybutton bacteria cultured to help in this discovery. The graphs are the findings of the research.
